Day 2

Live AI with no run model

Most organizations that reach production have no operating model for what comes next. Agents drift, grounding goes stale, costs creep and quality erodes silently. AgentOps is the discipline that keeps a live AI system trustworthy.

What's included

What this service covers

Quality & latency monitoring

Continuous monitoring of answer quality, latency and failure modes, with alerting before users feel it.

Evaluation & regression testing

Automated evals and regression suites so every change is measured, not hoped, before it ships.

Release control

Controlled, reversible releases for prompts, models, grounding and integrations — no silent changes in production.

Grounding content refresh

Keep the knowledge behind the agent current, governed and correctly scoped as your business changes.

Access reviews & cost optimization

Periodic access reviews plus token, model and routing optimization to keep spend proportionate to value.

Incident response & value reporting

Defined response for AI incidents, plus a monthly report tying quality, cost and usage to business outcomes.
