What this service covers

Use-case discovery

Structured workshops with business and IT to surface candidate use cases across functions — and separate the fundable from the fashionable.

Value & feasibility scoring

Each use case scored on business value, technical feasibility, data dependency and risk, so prioritization is a number, not a hunch.

Data readiness assessment
